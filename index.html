Who am I?

My name is Tanel Märjama and I'm a software developer located in Tartu, Estonia. I've been active in the IT field for over three years. My journey began as a computer network technician/administrator where I completed a one-year course at Tartu Vocational College (VOCO). After that I decided to continue my studies in IT field as a programmer and in spring 2023 I finished a two year course at VOCO as a software developer.
Writing code has always intrigued me, because it gives ma a challenge and there's always something new to learn. My aspiration is to become a good and dependable programmer in my field.
Besides programming I like to study music, play sports and work on cars.
